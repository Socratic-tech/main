How to Play

Round 1 teaches the difference between review, academic analysis, and weak / needs revision. Round 2 shifts to gaming history from the 1980s through the 2020s while still reinforcing mechanics, narrative, culture, and historical context.

Teacher Reminder

Keep pressing students on why. A review usually evaluates quality or recommends a game. Academic analysis interprets how a game creates meaning, makes an argument, and uses evidence. Some clues are deliberately weak so students can revise them into stronger claims.
