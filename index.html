About John

John Phillips is an EdTech innovator, author, and workshop leader whose work centers on a simple idea: when the environment is doing the work, students rise.

He designs ecosystems where belonging comes first, agency grows naturally, and learning becomes something students own. His work in esports, makerspaces, gameful learning, and hands-on tech environments highlights a consistent truth: students aren’t disengaged — the environment is.

Named the Most Innovative Professional Development Provider in the Midwest by Tech & Learning, John blends research-backed insight with a confident, playful style that resonates with educators and students alike.
