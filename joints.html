      Project Connections

        Vocabulary

        • Glue surface: Area where glue can bond two pieces together.
        • Shoulder: The flat step or ledge created by a cut.
        • Racking: Side-to-side force that twists a frame out of square.
        • End grain: The exposed ends of wood fibers. It usually glues weaker than long grain.
        • Dry fit: Test assembly before applying glue.
