How this trainer works

1. Notice the pattern

Learners see a reusable frame, not a giant grammar lecture.

2. Build from English

The prompt starts with an English idea. The learner turns it into Spanish using the frame.

3. Explain the miss

Wrong answers point back to the pattern: word order, trigger, verb form, or missing connector.

Teacher notes

What this is

This is a single-file Spanish trainer built around pattern recognition, sentence frames, short drills, and immediate feedback. It is meant for adults or secondary students who benefit from seeing the logic behind Spanish instead of memorizing isolated phrases.

What this is not

This is not an official Qroo Paul resource, not a replacement for his lessons, and not a copy of his paid materials. It uses original examples and common Spanish grammar patterns that are widely taught.

Best use

Use it for 5–10 minute warmups, independent practice, travel-prep drills, or Spanish I/II pattern reinforcement. The best flow is: introduce a pattern, let students play with the Pattern Lab, then assign 10 cards in Drill Mode.

How to share

Upload this HTML file to your website or Google Drive, or send it directly to a learner. It runs locally in the browser and does not need a server.

Accessibility and classroom controls

The tool uses large buttons, plain language, local saves, keyboard-friendly form fields, and printable teacher notes. For younger students, stick to cognates, sentence starters, and tile mode before using subjunctive drills.
