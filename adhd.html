The ADHD Brain

Regulation vs. Motivation

ADHD is a disorder of regulation, affecting focus, emotion, and executive function[cite: 13, 28].

• Interest-Based Decision Making: Driven by novelty/challenge, not importance[cite: 14].

• Dopamine Seeking: Brains seek dopamine and glucose to stay engaged[cite: 13].

The "Iceberg" Truth

Surface symptoms (hyperactivity/inattention) hide deeper challenges like RSD and sensory sensitivity[cite: 22, 23].
